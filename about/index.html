About PracticeNow

Helping teachers teach. Without the operations grind.

Without worries about filling classes, sharing Zoom links, managing attendance or following up for payments. Because they steal away the time and energy that you could spend on teaching itself.

Our story

All three of us — Vaidy, Leena and Janaki — are yoga students.

And each one of us has experienced immense value in the physical, mental and emotional wellbeing that yoga provides.

Each of us has also felt the struggle to stick with our practice regularly — in spite of the benefits we'd already seen.

Why is that? Why do we hesitate to show up on the mat, even when the benefits are already proven by our own personal experience?

That question drove us to create PracticeNow.

Most students have a long list of reasons (excuses!) to skip practice. Our goal with PracticeNow is to do away with as many of those reasons as we can — for both the student and their teacher.

Today, as soon as a student is interested in trying a class, she can hop onto our platform, pay for a class and join the class with one click. The teacher gets her fee, knows her name, and can welcome her back next week.

But like the yoga journey of that student that has just begun, our journey at PracticeNow is just getting started.

What will it take for the student to stick with the practice? What will it take for her to spread the word and grow the community around her teacher?

As we answer those questions over the coming months and years, we're certain of one thing — bringing consistency to students' lives starts with making it easy, secure and joyful for their teachers to teach.

Onward!
